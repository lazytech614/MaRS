Awards and Recognitions

Best Design Award

This award recognizes the robot with the most innovative, well-engineered, and aesthetically pleasing design. It considers factors such as functionality, efficiency, and creativity in design.

Technical Excellence Award

This award is given to the team that demonstrates outstanding technical proficiency in the design, construction, and programming of their robot. It recognizes teams that have implemented advanced techniques and achieved exceptional performance.

Innovation Award

The Innovation Award celebrates teams that have developed unique and groundbreaking solutions to the challenges presented in the competition. It recognizes creativity, ingenuity, and the ability to think outside the box in solving problems.
