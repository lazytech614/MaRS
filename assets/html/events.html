Events

Line Following Robot Challenge

Participants design and build autonomous robots capable of following a predefined path marked by a black line on a white surface. The robot that completes the course in the shortest time or with the highest accuracy wins.

Obstacle Avoidance Challenge

In this event, robots are tasked with navigating through a maze filled with obstacles. The goal is to program the robot to autonomously detect and avoid obstacles while reaching the end of the maze in the fastest time.

Sumo Robot Competition

Two robots compete in a sumo-style arena with the objective of pushing the opponent out of the ring. The robots are typically designed to be compact and powerful, equipped with sensors and motors to detect and push the opponent.

Robotic Arm Manipulation Challenge

Teams build robotic arms capable of completing specific tasks, such as picking and placing objects or assembling simple structures. The challenge may involve precision tasks that test the robot's dexterity and control.

Quadcopter Drone Racing

Participants design and pilot quadcopter drones through a challenging obstacle course. The event combines elements of engineering, programming, and piloting skills as participants compete for the fastest lap times.

Humanoid Robot Dance

Teams program humanoid robots to perform choreographed dance routines. The robots' movements, synchronization, and creativity are judged, making it a fun and entertaining event that showcases both technical skill and artistic expression.
