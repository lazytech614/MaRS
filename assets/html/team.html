Our Team

Our team is a diverse group of passionate individuals united by our love for robotics and innovation. Comprised of students from various disciplines, including engineering, computer science, and design, we bring together a range of skills and perspectives to tackle complex challenges in the field of robotics. With a strong emphasis on collaboration, creativity, and hands-on learning, we strive to push the boundaries of what is possible in robotics while fostering a supportive and inclusive community.
